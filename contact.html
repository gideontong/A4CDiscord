Need Help?

Need help? Although prayer can work wonders, sometimes it's as simple as a feature suggestion or an error message.

Have an issue?

No problem! Simply shoot an email to help@christalive.online and I'll get back to you within 5 business days. Want faster support? Become a Premium Tier supporter of Bot4Christ and recieve up to 10x faster support (depending on time zone). Alternatively, if you know exactly what is wrong, feel free to file a report on the issue tracker instead.

Have a suggestion?

Great to hear! Shoot an email to suggestions@christalive.online and I'll definitely take a look, although I can't guarantee I'll respond. New feature requests are being worked on all the time, and if you are a Premium Tier supporter of my work I am more than happy to give your suggestion a priority look.
